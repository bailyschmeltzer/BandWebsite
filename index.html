About Us

Neon Oblivion is a hard-hitting rock and metal cover duo bringing raw power and electrifying energy to every performance. Formed by two musicians with a shared passion for the genre’s most iconic heavyweights, we channel the spirit of bands like Metallica, Linkin Park, Rage Against the Machine, Ghost, and more—reviving the anthems that defined generations and reimagining them with our own edge. Whether it’s the crushing riffs of thrash, the aggressive grooves of nu-metal, or the theatrical punch of modern rock, our mission is simple: to make every set unforgettable. We don't just play the songs—we relive them, with the same intensity and emotion that made them legendary. Currently based Columbus Ohio, Neon Oblivion is all about stripped-down, high-impact performances that prove two people can still make a stadium-sized sound.
